The Character Build Calculator has been updated to NWN 1.69.

- Epic Dodge requires Tumble 30 (instead of just Tumble 1),
- Dragon Shape requires Shape 6x/day (instead of Shape 5x/day),
- Epic Weapon Focus Club requires Weapon Focus Club (instead of Impr. Crit. Club),
- Weapon Specialization Club requires at least four fighter levels,
- Trident Weapon Feats (WF, EWF, WS, EWS, Impr. Crit., Overwh. Crit., Dev. Crit., Weapon of Choice), Trident is a valid weapon for CoT and WM.

CoT still can't take Ambidexterity/TWF as bonus feats but that will be fixed in v. 2.831.

If I've missed something let me know.
