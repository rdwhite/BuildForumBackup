Care to justify Ranger 10 over Fighter 4-6? Sure. The main reason is Ranger gets Dual Wield and Improved Two-weapon Fighting feats free. A fighter build requires Ambidexterity and 15 dexterity to get that feat.

You'd have to drop too many points from wisdom, strength, intelligence and con to get the required 15 Dex.

Another minor bonus is the wisdom required for Cleric spells opens up level 2 Ranger spells, so you can buff Hide & Move silently with Camouflage and One with the Land. Although obviously not much of a sneaker
